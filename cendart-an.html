Project description

This project is the result of a request from the smoking students of the Adimaker school.

The goal was to create a structure to collect cigarette butts. In order to use our project, we wanted to playful gesture of throwing a butt. For this, we use a screen and two sensors, present in each of the columns of our structure. On the screen, a question was displayed, and changed every week. When a butt is detected, a vote is taken into account.

Our structure is combined by a solar panel, which allows it to be self sufficient.
